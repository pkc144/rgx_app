## üé≠ Behind the Scenes - What Happens Automatically

### When You Open the App

**What you see:** Home screen loads

**What happens behind the scenes:**
1. App checks if you're logged in (like Facebook remembering you)
2. Fetches your latest recommendations from the server
3. Gets your current portfolio values
4. Loads educational content
5. Checks for new notifications
6. Updates your account balance
7. All this happens in 1-2 seconds!

**Real-world analogy:** Like a restaurant preparing your table before you arrive

### When You Get a Notification

**What you see:** A notification on your phone

**What happens behind the scenes:**
1. Your advisor creates a recommendation on their system
2. Server sends a message to Firebase (Google's notification service)
3. Firebase sends the notification to your phone
4. Your phone shows the notification
5. App stores the recommendation in your account
6. When you tap, app fetches full details
7. All this happens in milliseconds!

**Real-world analogy:** Like a postal service delivering a letter to your mailbox

### When You Execute a Trade

**What you see:** "Order placed successfully"

**What happens behind the scenes:**
1. You tap "Execute Trade"
2. App sends request to the server
3. Server checks your broker connection
4. Server places order with your broker (Zerodha, etc.)
5. Broker executes the trade on the stock exchange
6. Broker sends confirmation back to server
7. Server updates your app
8. You see the confirmation
9. All this happens in 2-5 seconds!

**Real-world analogy:** Like ordering food on Swiggy - you tap, restaurant gets order, food is prepared, delivered to you

## üéì Key Concepts Explained Simply

**Simple explanation:** A recipe for investing

**Example:**
- Recipe: "Balanced Portfolio"
- Ingredients: 40% Large companies, 30% Mid-size companies, 20% Bonds, 10% Gold

**Simple explanation:** Adjusting your investments to match the original plan

**Example:**
- You started with 50% Stock A and 50% Stock B
- Stock A grew, now it's 70% of your portfolio
- Rebalancing: Sell some Stock A, buy more Stock B

**Simple explanation:** Custom recommendations made just for you

**Example:**
- Not a pre-made portfolio
- Your advisor picks individual stocks for you
- Like: A tailor making a custom suit vs. buying off the rack

### What is "Stop Loss"?

**Example:**
- You buy a stock at ‚Çπ100
- Set stop loss at ‚Çπ90
- If price drops to ‚Çπ90, automatically sell
- You lose only ‚Çπ10, not more
- Like: A circuit breaker that stops electricity when there's a problem

**Example:**
- You buy at ‚Çπ100

## ü§î Common Questions Beginners Ask

### "Why do I need to connect my broker?"
**Answer:** Think of it like this:
- The app is your **advisor** (tells you what to do)
- Your broker is your **executor** (actually does it)
- You need both: advice + execution
- Like: A doctor (advisor) and a pharmacy (executor)

### "What if I don't understand a recommendation?"
**Answer:** You have options:
1. Read the educational content first
2. Ignore recommendations you don't understand
3. Start with small amounts to learn
4. Contact your advisor for clarification
- Like: Not ordering a dish you don't understand on a menu

### "How do I know if a recommendation is good?"
**Answer:** Look for:
- Clear target price (where to sell for profit)
- Stop loss (protection from big losses)
- Reasoning from your advisor
- Past performance of similar recommendations
- Like: Reading restaurant reviews before trying a new place

### "What happens if I miss a notification?"
**Answer:** Don't worry!
- All recommendations stay in your app
- You can view them anytime
- They don't disappear until you act on them
- Like: Missed calls - you can see them later

### "Can I undo a trade?"
**Answer:** Once executed, no
- Stock market trades are final
- That's why there's a confirmation step
- Always review before confirming
- Like: Sending an email - once sent, can't unsend
