## ğŸ“ Glossary - Terms Explained Simply

**Stock:** A piece of ownership in a company (like owning a slice of pizza)

**Portfolio:** Collection of all your investments (like your wardrobe)

**Buy:** Purchasing shares (like buying apples)

**Sell:** Selling shares you own (like selling your old phone)

**Target Price:** Price to sell for profit (sell concert tickets for more)

**Stop Loss:** Safety price to prevent big losses (airbag in a car)

**Rebalance:** Adjusting portfolio to match plan (balancing a seesaw)

**Model Portfolio:** Pre-made investment strategy (pre-set workout plan)

**Bespoke:** Custom-made for you (tailored suit)

**Broker:** Company that executes trades (real estate agent)
